Sr. Pastor Sathish Philip and
Feby Sathish

Pastor Sathish Philip, born on July 5, 1978, as the youngest son of late Mr. Thomas Philip and Mrs. Thankamma, is the senior pastor of Church of God Gospel Center, Palarivattom, Kochi. When Pr. Sathish was 24, he had an encounter with the Lord Jesus and gave his life completely to serve the Lord. For more than 20 years, Pr. Sathish has helped multitudes realize the purpose of their life.
He is a loving husband to Feby Sathish. Feby, born on 10/10/1982, as the first daughter of Pr. N. G. Samkutty and Mariyamma, a best friend, a mother, a loving wife, and is Pastor Sathish's better helpmate as well. Feby and Sathish have managed every ministry opportunity, as well as personal ventures, with their unwavering faith in God. The beautiful couple is successfully raising three precious children, Ben Eliyahu Philip, Sam Elisha Philip, and Jerusha Ruth Philip.
Pr. Sathish and Feby, with one accord, say, "How true intimacy with God is; we feel that no one else can really understand the special bond that we share with him because it's altogether personal." "We cannot describe the beauty of our relationship with Christ." The secret of their marital bliss is their intimacy with Jesus, and they support each other every step of the way.

Moreover, Feby Sathish is the passionate creator behind the up-and-coming YouTube channel "Rose of Sharon." On this platform, she shares inspiring and insightful content centered around her deep faith in God. Through her videos, Feby endeavors to foster a profound connection with Jesus, aiming to guide and uplift her audience on their spiritual journey. The channel serves as a virtual haven for those seeking a closer relationship with the divine, as Feby beautifully articulates her experiences and revelations in the pursuit of a more profound love for Christ.
