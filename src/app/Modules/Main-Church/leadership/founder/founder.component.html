Founding Pastor
N. G. Samkutty (Late) and
Mariyamma Sam

Late Pr. N. G. Samkutty was born on November 27th, 1956 to Jacobite parents in Pathanamthitt, India. He is the youngest son of the family. Mrs Mariyamma Sam, wife of Pr Samkutty was born on November 24th 1957 to Jacobite parents in Pathanapuram India. She is the fourth Daughter of the family.
They both are sent by God and are unique ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ. Their spirit filled and anointed leadership helped them to lead a dynamic, global ministry. As a pastor, teach, healing minister and prophet, Pastor Samkutty reached the people of the world with God's manifest presence.
As an extra ordinary man of faith, the story of Pastor Samkutty's life was inspiring and can be summed up with a simple but telling passage from Hebrew 10:38 "Now the just shall live by faith; but if any man draw back, my soul shall have no pleasure in him".
Pastor Sam began his ministry by leading prayers for a few and later he plated many churches in various parts of India. He has a personal encounter with the lord and named the church "Church of God Gospel Centre". He could train thousands of missionaries in and out of India. When he was 52 years, he got called back to the eternal home.
Pastor Mariyamma Sam is carrying vision and been involved in the ministry.
