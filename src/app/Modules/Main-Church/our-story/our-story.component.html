One Church - Church of God

In the heart of Kochi, a tapestry of faith, commitment, and spiritual growth unfolds—the story of the Church of God Gospel Centre. Our journey began in 1995 when we established our foundation as a place of worship, fellowship, and spiritual enlightenment.

From those humble beginnings, we experienced transformative milestones. In the year 2000, with a growing congregation and unwavering faith, we moved to our own premises. This shift marked a significant chapter in our story, as we continued to nurture a thriving community rooted in love and devotion.

The establishment of the Cochin Bible College in the same year became a testament to our commitment to in-depth biblical education and theological exploration. As our community grew, so did our vision, leading to the inception of Ahavah Kids Church in 2016—a vibrant space for our youngest members to connect with their faith.

As the world embraced digital communication, so did we. In 2020, Ahavah Media came to life, leveraging the power of media to spread our message of love, hope, and faith. Simultaneously, Ahavah Home team opened its doors, providing a nurturing educational environment that integrates faith and learning.

Our story is deeply intertwined with visionary leaders, Pastor N. G. Samkutty and Mrs. Mariyamma Sam. Born in the mid-20th century to Jacobite parents, Pastor Samkutty emerged as an extraordinary man of faith. His ministry, marked by an encounter with the Lord, led to the establishment of the "Church of God Gospel Centre." His legacy includes planting numerous churches and training thousands of missionaries.

Pastor Samkutty's journey was not only one of faith but of dedication to God's work. His story, summarized by Hebrew 10:38, reflects a life lived entirely by faith. At the age of 52, he was called back to the eternal home, leaving behind a legacy that continues to shape our mission.

Carrying the torch forward, Pastor Sathish Philip, born in 1978, became the senior pastor of the Church of God Gospel Centre. His encounter with Jesus at 24 propelled him into a life dedicated to serving the Lord. For over 20 years, Pastor Sathish has guided multitudes to realize the purpose of their lives.

Alongside him is Feby Sathish, a vital part of our leadership and ministry. Their partnership extends beyond the church walls as a loving couple, parents to Ben Eliyahu Philip, Sam Elisha Philip, and Jerusha Ruth Philip. In unity, they express the deep intimacy with God that guides their lives.

Feby Sathish, in her role as the creator of the YouTube channel "Rose of Sharon," shares inspiring content rooted in deep faith. This platform serves as a virtual haven, fostering a profound connection with Jesus and guiding others on their spiritual journey.

Today, the ministry is blessed to have dedicated pastors, including Pastors Joseph Cleetus, Thomas Varghese, and Mariyamma Sam. Their leadership adds depth and diversity to our spiritual journey. In addition to our pastoral team, the Church of God Gospel Centre is enriched by dynamic ministry teams. Our dedicated Media , Ahavah Home, and Worship teams contribute tirelessly to the vibrant life of our church. Their commitment enhances our worship experience, community outreach, and the use of media for spreading the Gospel. As we continue to grow and evolve, these teams remain integral to our mission, reinforcing our commitment to faith, service, and community building. Together, pastors and teams, we stand united, shaping the future chapters of our shared journey.
